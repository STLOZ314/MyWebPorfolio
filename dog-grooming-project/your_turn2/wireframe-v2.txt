Dog Grooming Website Wireframe (Updated)
Date: 2026-05-27

Global Layout
1. Header
   - Business name (H1)
   - Short tagline

2. Navigation (horizontal on desktop, stacked on mobile)
   - Home
   - Services
   - Pricing
   - Contact

3. Main Content
   - Hero image and welcome text on Home page
   - Service cards (3 columns desktop, 1 column mobile) on Services page
   - Price table on Pricing page
   - Contact details and image on Contact page

4. Footer
   - Copyright line

Responsive Behavior
- Images scale to available width.
- Navigation switches from inline links to stacked links on small screens.
- Service cards stop floating and stack vertically on small screens.

Visual Direction
- High contrast palette: deep blue, white, and yellow hover accents.
- Clear typography and spacing for readability.
