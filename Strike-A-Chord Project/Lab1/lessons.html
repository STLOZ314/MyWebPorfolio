Music Lessons

Strike a Chord offers lessons for piano, guitar, and violin. We offer lessons for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced students. Our experienced instructors are dedicated to helping you reach your musical goals. Your first two lessons are FREE!

Our piano lessons are available for students of all ages and skill levels. Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced pianist, our instructors will help you develop your technique and musicianship. Lessons are available in 30- and 60-minute sessions.
